How it works

Simple by design,
powerful under the hood.

No account beyond Google. No monthly fee. Your data lives in a real Google Sheet you own — forever.

01

Sign in with Google

One tap and FairShare creates a Google Sheet in your Drive for each trip. You own the data.

02

Log expenses on the go

Add who paid, who's splitting, and how much. Works fully offline — syncs when back online.

03

Balances update instantly

All calculations happen on-device. No server round trips, no loading spinners.

04

Settle with fewer transfers

FairShare's algorithm minimises the total number of payments to settle everyone up cleanly.

Why FairShare

Built different.

Born out of frustration with being asked to pay a monthly subscription just to split a dinner bill. FairShare does the same thing, but keeps your data in your own Google Drive. No server, no company holding your receipts, no lock-in. Just you and a spreadsheet you actually own.

🔒

Zero server dependency

Your data never touches a FairShare server. Google Sheets is the only backend — and it's yours, always.

⚖️

Minimum transactions

Smart settlement algorithm that reduces the number of payments everyone has to make to zero out.

🆓

Free. Forever.

No subscription. No ads. No upsell. FairShare is a personal project built to be genuinely useful.
